A tool that parses through your emails to let you know what school clubs have food/what is
happening and marks it on your calendar
thin-slice
1. Who is the user?
  Primary user:
  A college student who:
  Gets lots of school/club emails. Which once it gets popular would make students want to
  sign up for as many clubs as possible since staying informed would be so accessible
  Misses events because they’re buried in inbox noise
  Cares about free food, interesting events, or easy-to-attend stuff
  Uses Google Calendar / Apple Calendar / etc regularly
  Doesn’t want to hunt through emails or social media to find what’s happening
  Secondary (later): Club organizers who want better turnout and less wasted food.

2. What’s the problem?
  Students:
  Inbox is too full with other things or previous announcements
  Miss events they would’ve gone to if they had noticed them
  Especially miss free food events and low-effort campus activities
  Don’t want to manually put things in their calendar
  So the core problem is: Good campus events are hidden in my inbox (later: social media), and
  it’s annoying to extract and remember them. Basically people would go to more events
  (especially free food ones) if they were automatically pulled out of my email and put on my
  calendar and were easier to pay for online instead of having to use cash.

3. One end-to-end journey (thin slice)
  1. One simple success path:
  2. Student signs in with their school Gmail
  3. App scans recent emails
  4. App finds an email like:
    a. “Pizza Night – Wednesday 7pm, Lowenstein 302
  5. App shows:
    a. Event title
    b. Date/time
    c. Location Badge (an emoji makes it playful): 🍕 “Free food”
  6. Student clicks “Add to Calendar”
7. Event appears on their Google Calendar

4. What is explicitly out of scope (for this thin slice)? This would usually mean what we’re not
building now but read it over if its something you would want for today lmk.
  Payments / ticketing
  Club dashboards or organizer tools
  RSVP systems
  Attendance tracking
  Push notifications
  AI “smart recommendations”
  Social features (sharing, friends, etc.)
  Editing events
  Writing emails or posting for clubs
  Supporting multiple email providers (start with one, e.g., Gmail)
  Auto-adding events without user click (manual click first)
